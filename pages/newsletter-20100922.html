Harvest

This year we have an outstanding number of parents joining us for lunch. The full details and arrangements will be on a separate letter. Money raised will be split between Weston Hospicecare and Woodford Primary School

* Woodford Primary will use any money raised to buy seeds which they will plant themselves in order to grow food for their lunches.

* Weston Hospicecare would be very very grateful to receive food donations - every item will be used, so fresh or tinned goods can be brought in on 30th September.

* Please encourage your children to take part. Thank you in advance on behalf of all those who will benefit from your generosity.
