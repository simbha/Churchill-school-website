Maintianing the Village Hall

Many of you have children who will have attended Churchill Pre-School, or take part in activities at Churchill Memorial Hall. Although inside the hall is in good decorative order, the roof, much of it still the original, (built in 1926), desperately needs renewing. We have almost enough money to start work this August and are still fundraising to reach our target. A Promise Auction is being held at the hall on Saturday 19th May – 10am for viewing, bidding starts at 10.30am. Admission is free and there will be homemade refreshments available. Please come along- if only for the experience and a piece of cake! Alternatively, if you can’t make it and you have a gift/skill/service you can offer for auction I would be pleased to hear from you. A Programme of Lots should be available from Churchill Post Office from 10th May.

If you would like any further information please contact me, Anne Williams, on 01934 852430 or email dferrierwilliams@tiscali.co.uk. Your Hall needs your support.

Riding Centre Open Day

Little Grange Riding Centre is a well established and respected riding school which has been running for many years, and for the first time, is inviting everyone along to visit this fun and friendly community at an Open Day on Saturday 12th May. If you love horses and ponies, and would like to learn a little more about them or would just like to see a riding school in operation, then you will enjoy this opportunity. Admission is free, so bring all the family along for an interesting day out. Activities on offer include meeting the horses and ponies, watching lessons for various ages and abilities, help to groom a pony, tack demonstrations, learn about the various parts of a horse, and even be shown what a horse eats. There will also be kiddie colouring competitions, tombola and lucky dip. Beverages, hot dogs, snacks, cakes and ice cream will be on offer throughout the day. You would be made very welcome. The gates will open to all visitors at 9.30am until 4.00pm. So be sure to visit Little Grange Riding Centre, Brockley Way, Claverham BS49 4PA and enjoy the day. For more information call Little Grange on 01934 832558.
