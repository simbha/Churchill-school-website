Year 4 Bristol Museum Trip

We enjoyed the trip to Bristol museum and art gallery last Wednesday. It was interesting being archaeologists and feeling the Egyptian objects in the workshop. We liked seeing the Egyptian mummies and couldn’t believe they were 2,500 years old! We had an extraordinary day learning lots of mind blowing facts.

Governors Profile – Mrs. Lloyd, Teacher Governor

I have been a teacher governor for over six years. As a Deputy Head and member of the teaching staff I am constantly part of school life and enjoy being involved in the decisions required for the school to run efficiently. I sit on the Curriculum committee as my teaching position within school has provided me with a sound knowledge and expertise of the curriculum. I have two grown up children who have both gone through university. My hobbies include sport and gardening—in particular, growing and then eating my own vegetables.
