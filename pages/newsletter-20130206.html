Heart Foundation

A total of £82.09 was raised last week. Every penny raised will help in the fight against heart and circulatory disease. Some of the ways the money is used for this vital work is buying assay kits, supplying nurses, scientists and even funding teenagers on a weekend of support and confidence building. Thank you for your support.

Maths Countdown – Thursday 7th February 7.00pm

Have you ever worried about helping your children with their maths? Then this evening is for you. We will begin in the hall with an introduction about maths teaching across the school. We will then rotate around the classrooms learning about a different maths operation in each room. By the end of the evening we will all know how addition/subtraction/multiplication/division are taught across the school.

Pancake Day

On Thursday, 7th February we will be making pancakes in school to celebrate Shrove Tuesday, marking the beginning of Lent. Year 5 and 6 will be sharing their Lent book, donated by our village churches, with all pupils. These books suggest ideas of what you can ‘do’ rather than give up for lent e.g. phone someone you love but haven’t seen for a while or have a TV/computer game-free day and do something different instead. We look forward to hearing what you did!
