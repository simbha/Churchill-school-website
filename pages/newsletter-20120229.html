Reading & writing national trends

National trends show a decline in children’s reading and writing skills. In school we work hard to create an environment where children are surrounded by ideas to encourage thinking and talking. You can help by reading regularly with your child and discussing stories, poems and news items. Sometimes children’s lives are so busy that children miss out on the simple things in life—learning days of week, names of fruit or vegetables etc. So maybe some ‘old fashioned’ time is needed; who’s up for a country walk this weekend?
