Fish

A huge thank you to Cadbury Garden Centre for the donations of fish, jelly fish and “bones”! The children have been fascinated by the variety of glittering fish that are now swimming amongst the plants.

It is Walk to School Day this Friday; please encourage your children to take part in this event to help them work towards a healthier life. Parking available at the Stag and Hounds
