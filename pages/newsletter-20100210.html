Active Kids Vouchers

We are now collecting Sainsbury’s Active Kid’s vouchers so if you do your shopping in their supermarket, please remember to collect your vouchers and bring them to school. We have been able to purchase some really good equipment in years gone by so it is well worth taking part.

Jewellery Recycling

Turn your unwanted items into a donation for our school! Please donate your unwanted gold, silver, costume jewellery and watches towards this recycling appeal. Damaged, broken and incomplete items will help so all those odd earrings and broken chains are a great start! Everything you give us is turned into cash for our school, recycled and put to good use!
