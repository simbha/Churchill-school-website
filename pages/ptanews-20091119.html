PTA Newsletter - 19th November 2009

Christmas Fayre Update

The Christmas Fayre is fast approaching and we thought a reminder list of what's needed when might be helpful;

Tomorrow

Could hamper items be in by tomorrow (Friday 20th) please. Each class has a box for hamper items.

Next Friday (27th November) Mufti Day

Tombola items - There will be a mufti day in exchange for tombola items on Friday 27th November. This year in addition to our normal tombola we will be running a children's tombola stall. Could you bring an item for either (or both). A suggestion for the children's tombola stall is to decorate a glass jar (jam jar size) containing a small toy, marbles etc. Mrs Strawbridge will be showing the children some examples this week.

Books & Toys

If you have any good quality books or toys for the fayre could you bring them in to school in the week leading up to the fayre.

Friday 4th December

Cakes for the cake stall please.

Stalls at Xmas Fayre

We have a variety of stall holders attending the fayre but we've still room for a few more so if you know of someone who may be interested in having a stall please contact Katy Pearce on 01934 853094.

Stall Rota

I'm sure you will have been approached by a PTA class rep asking if you could spare just half an hour to help at the fayre. We really can't run the stalls without your help and if everyone offers a little time then it's not an onerous task. Please try and help if you can.

Promise Auction

Promises to be in by Monday 23rd November (next Monday). At our Xmas fayre we will be holding another promise auction. Over the past few years the promise auction has been highly successful but it does rely upon everyone's help. if you know of any companies or organisations that might donate something for the auction the could you please contact a member of the PTA or speak with Mrs Joskey (year 6). We will also be looking for the sort of promises that we can all help out with for example; a evening babysitting, baking a cake for special occasion; a few hours gardening - virtually anything goes so please help us out with an item or two for the promise auction it will be much appreciated.

Other

The school has a number of educational videos that they would like to transfer to DVD format if anyone has a piece of equipment that they would be happy to loan to school for a short while would you please contact the school office. Many thanks.

We appreciate there is a lot going on over the coming weeks but we really do need your help to ensure that the Christmas Fayre is a success. We very much appreciate your support. Thank you.
