Parents Working Party

The Coffee Bite/Chunk for this week is…

Marking children's work is very important because It help them to know how well they are doing and what they need to do next. If your child is in Year R, 1 or 2 you will often see a tick and 'v' on their work, this means the teacher has discussed their work and told them how to make it "even better".

In Year 3, 4, 5 and 6 you will often see written comments from the teacher - EBI (even better if) and then the children follow the advice from the teacher and update the work.

WWW (what went well) comment tells the children their strengths success and how well they are doing.

Children put smiley or sad faces on their work to indicate whether they understood or not. Sometimes children "mark" each others work and offer helpful comments.

Maths work may sometimes be marked by the class with the teacher then looking at the marking in the evening.

If you want to find out more about marking, the policy will be up on the website soon. In the meantime TTT (talk to teacher) or your child.
