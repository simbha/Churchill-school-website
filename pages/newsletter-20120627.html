Fact Of The Week

This week the invention of the toothbrush was celebrated. Emily and Sophie-Marie in Year 5 found out that the early toothbrushes are mentioned as far back as 3000 B.C. when the Egyptians and Babylonians used toothbrushes called ‘chew sticks’. The first bristle toothbrushes were invented in China in 1498 where boar bristles were attached to handles of bamboo or bone.
