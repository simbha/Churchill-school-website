PTA Summer Fair is Huge Success

Thank you to everyone who came and enjoyed the summer fair, we raised around £2,000, a fantastic achievement. A special thank you to all the adults and children who helped to set up on Friday night and take down on Sunday. I have great admiration for the PTA Committee, they spent many hours planning and preparing before the fair. Their hard work and dedication underpinned the event and ensured an amazing day for our village community – thank you to each of them.

Note from your Governors

This year we have welcomed on to the governing body Nick Hobson, Lorna Vickery, Corinne Higgins and, very recently, Ben Cuckson and have said farewell to Wyc Slingsby, Lynne Lloyd, Leigh Machell, Mary Smallwood, Roger Humm, and Jonathan Clamp. We are grateful to them all, wish them well, and warn all parents that we are always on the look-out for new governors to join us!

The committees have been working hard as usual. The mammoth task of managing the finances and staffing requirements of the school is in the capable hands of the Management Committee. This year has seen the recruitment of four new class teachers, one part-time deputy head teacher, a bursar and an office administrator, so the Personnel Committee has also been kept very busy. The Facilities Committee have been masterminding the project to improve the playground and field area, and we are very excited that some of the work will be taking place over the summer holidays. Watch that space! In the meantime the Curriculum Committee have been familiarising themselves with the new Safeguarding regulations which have been introduced to protect our children as well as taking an active role in assessing the provision of the various curriculum subjects within our school.

The every day business of reviewing policies, attending governors’ meetings and supporting all the school’s activities goes on behind the scenes. Please contact any of us if you have any concerns or an interest in making a further contribution to this great school.
