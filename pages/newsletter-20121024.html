School Council

Last week the school council met and talked about how to make the school better. They all spoke to their own classes on what they wanted to change or make better. PTA News!

The PTA committee met for the first time this week, and they would like to thank the outgoing committee, particularly Katie, Denise and Justin for all their help and support.

The Christmas Fayre 2012 will take place on Saturday 8th December from 12.00 noon -4.00pm, so don't forget to make a note for your diary. This year we will have a prize for the best decorated class hamper, so please sign up to donate an item after half term (there will be a list outside each classroom). The fantastic Promise Auction will also return so please think about the promise you could donate - anything from baking a cake, babysitting to a weekend away - the sky's the limit! If you know of any companies or local business’ who could donate a promise that would be great too. There will be an open meeting for all parents to discuss the Christmas Fair on Thursday 8th November from 2pm. If you would like to get involved but can't attend the meeting, please speak to your class rep, any member of the PTA, Liz or Jackie in the office. All parents are automatically a member of the PTA, so please feel free to come along to any meeting. We will also be using the PTA noticeboard (on the left as you enter the playground) so don't forget to keep an eye out for the exciting fundraising events planned for this school year. Thank you in advance for all your support - we are looking forward to raising money to benefit our children and our school.
