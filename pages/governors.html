One is a doctor and the other an accountant and I am sure that their success has been built on the foundations of their time at Churchill Primary.

Areas of responsibility

I am currently serving as Chair of the Governing Body, and am part of the Management Committee. I also have special interest in the teaching of maths.

Other Information

My interest in science, food and nutrition led me to choose Dietetics as a profession. I work at Weston General Hospital, where I manage a team of 10 Dieticians, and in my spare time, love gardening, running and all my menagerie of animals. Why am I a Governor? Because of the children, and because it is a wonderful school. I hope I can play a small part in its successful future development.
