Everybody Writes Day 2012 – The Owl

Next Monday we will be joined by some feathery friends! With the help of Darren Jenkins aka ‘The Owl Man’, children will discover a little more about the six species of owl on display. We also hope this fantastic experience will stimulate some powerful persuasive writing later on in the day!

e-Safety Evening

Have You Lost Anything?

We have a number of items in the school office which have been found and handed in. These include keys and glasses so if you think you have mislaid anything please ask. Any other items of lost property may be in the green bin outside Year 2.

Festive Fun Run

Exciting plans are underway for our Festive Fun Run on 8th December. It will be a fun event for all the family – children and adults will be able to take part. We will be sending home registration forms and more information after the half term break. In the meantime, keep up your training.

Churchill Pre-School Disco

The Pre-school will be running a fancy dress “Spooky Disco” for all pre-schoolers and toddlers on Tuesday, 23rd October from 4.30-6.00pm. Tickets £2.50 to include a ‘sandwich box’ tea and homemade soup will be available to purchase for adults. They are hoping that this will become a regular feature so please support this event. For more information contact Sarah on 07530690399.

Christmas Shopping Evening

This North Somerset run Children’s Centre run various classes including baby clinics, ante natal classes and information on childcare, adult learning, employment and local information. For more details contact Debbie Pope on 01934 426643.

Weston Handmade & Gift Fair

Enchanted Fairs are running the Weston Handmade & Gift Fair with a mix of stalls from some very talented local people. This will be held at Weston Football Club on Sunday, 4th November from 10.00am to 4.00pm and supporting the Cross Roads Care for Young Carers.

The Giving Machine – Shop Online & Raise Funds For Our School

You can generate free cash donations for the school every time you shop online via The Giving Machine. Some of the retailers who subscribe to this include Amazon, tesco, Asda, Sainsburys, Next, John Lewis, PC World, Toys R Us, Vodafone and a lot, lot more.
