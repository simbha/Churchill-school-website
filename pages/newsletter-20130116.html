Churchill Post Office & Stores

“The Christmas Carol” event held at Churchill Post Office raised a huge £292 which was donated to Churchill/Langford Mother & Toddler Group and Weston Hopicecare. Sandra and Clive Heyward would like to thank everyone who came along, helped out, donated mince pies, sang and danced.

Messy Gang @ Churchill Methodist Church

Crafts and games, followed by a small service and tea, for children aged 4-8 years with the theme ‘Creation’ this Sunday, 20th January 2013 from 3.30-5.30pm. Children must be accompanied by an adult for this joyful event.

Village Fund Quiz Night

Will be held on Friday, 22nd February in our school hall. This promises to be an entertaining night with a ploughman’s supper. Money raised will be shared amongst the village communities e.g. Brownies.
