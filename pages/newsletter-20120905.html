Newsletter - 5th September 2012

Welcome back to a busy autumn term. I hope everyone enjoyed their summer break and were inspired by the Olympics. I am certainly going to try harder to go the gym once a week.

We will continue encouraging the children to enjoy sports through after school clubs and Mr. Mason, from Churchill Academy, teaching afternoons as well as our PE lessons including dance, swimming, gym and team games.

The children have made a delightful start to the year and are full of enthusiasm for school.

We welcome all our “new families” and wish you a happy and successful time with us.

Safety First

According to the Highway Code if you CYCLE to school with your child(ren) please make sure you follow the correct procedure on the road in following the same line as the traffic i.e. on the left. Anyone WALKING to school on roads with no pavements should walk facing the oncoming traffic.
