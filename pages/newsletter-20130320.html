Statatory Inspection Of Anglican Schools (SIAS)

Yesterday we had a SIAS inspection of our school. SIAS inspection reports provides a different but complementary perspective on church schools in relation to Ofsted’s findings. SIAS inspections may be either concurrent with the Ofsted Section 5 inspection or held after the Ofsted inspection. The principal objective of the inspection is to evaluate the distinctiveness and effectiveness of the school as a church school.

Towards this objective, inspectors seek answers to three key questions:-

  • How well does the school, through its distinctive Christian character, meet the needs of all learners?
  • What is the impact of collective worship on the school community?
  • How effective are the leadership and management of the school as a church school?

The report will be issued at the start of term 5 and we look forward to sharing the findings with you then.

Thank you to the staff, Governors, parents and children who spoke with the inspector and shared their views of Churchill Primary School.
