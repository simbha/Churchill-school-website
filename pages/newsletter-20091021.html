Newsletter - 21st October 2009

As this very busy term draws to a close, I hope you will enjoy the half-term break with your children amongst the leaves:

How silently they tumble down
And come to rest upon the ground
To lay a carpet rich and rare,
Beneath the trees without a care,
Content to sleep, their work well done,
Colours gleaming in the sun.

Maybe your family could write a "leafy" poem to bring back to school on November 2nd for our display.
