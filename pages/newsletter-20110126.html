Lunchtime supervisors

1 had a very successful meeting with the Lunchtime Supervisors this week, and we are going to be implementing some changes.

A group of Year 5 pupil volunteers will be trained to take on the role of "Playground Buddies". They will offer a variety of structured play activities at lunchtimes on a Tuesday and Friday. Parents working party

This weeks Coffee Bite is…

"What happens to my child at Lunchtime?"

At 12:15 Key Stage 1 children break for lunch and go to the Hall for their hot dinner or sandwiches. They are looked after by the dinner supervisors who encourage your child(ren) to eat as much of their hot lunch or sandwiches as they can.

During this time Key Stage 2 children finish their morning session. They go into the Hall for their lunch, around 12:50. During this time Key Stage 1 children have use of the playground.

At 13:00 Key Stage 1 and Key Stage 2 children play together until the end of lunch.

The dinner supervisors organise games and Playground Buddies will approach lone children to help them find a game to play. There are set days for each class to play games such as football and the playground is zoned, providing a range of areas such as quiet places.

Children can come into school to get a drink or visit the toilet, and indoor activities are organised when the weather stops them going outside. If you have any further questions please see a member of the Parent's Working Party or your class teacher.
