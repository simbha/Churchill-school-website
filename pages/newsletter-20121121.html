Anti-Bullying Week

Last week we joined thousands of children across the country to help raise awareness of the impact of bullying. In our classes we discussed, drew and debated what bullies look like, how it feels to be bullied and what we can do to stop bullying. The choir sang and danced “Raise My Voice”, an upbeat song with a positive message. The children enjoyed watching them so much they have been invited to teach the dance moves to KS1.
