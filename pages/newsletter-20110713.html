Blankets for Woodford

Our volunteer, Mrs. Pain, has been teaching the children to knit. They have knitted squares which have been sewn together to make blankets to send to Woodford Primary, our linked school in South Africa as it is now their winter. The Community School have also donated their blankets to send. The project will continue next year so if you are interested or know some ‘knitters’ please join us in September.
