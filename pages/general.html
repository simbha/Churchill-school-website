About Us - General

We are a C.E.V.C primary school, catering for children between the ages of 4 and 11. We are located in the West Country, in beautiful countryside at the foot of the Mendip Hills, and are maintained by North Somerset Council. Our head teacher's name is Mrs Tighe.

There are approximately 200 pupils on roll, most living within the community. The majority of pupils are expected to continue their education at Churchill Community School (comprehensive).
