National Child Measurement Programme (NCMP)

The NCMP is a joint initiative between the Department for Children, Schools and Families (DCSF) and the Department of Health (DH). It is part of a shared programme of work to improve children's health and wellbeing. The School Nursing Team will be visiting school during this academic year to weigh and measure children in Year 6 and Reception. Parents will receive a letter about this nearer to the time that the School Nursing Team will be visiting which will give more information about the NCMP. To find out more visit www.teachernet.gov.uk/wholeschool/healthyliving/obesity/ncmp

Starting School In September 2010

If you have a child who will be aged 4 between 1st September 2009 and 31st August 2010, then you need to apply for a school place. On 1st September 2009 a Guide for Parents became available on the website www.n-somerset.gov.uk/admissions which contains information about applying for school places for September 2010. You may apply online or alternatively an application form and guide is available from School Admissions and Transport Team, North Somerset Council, Town Hall, Walliscote Grove Road, Weston-super-Mare, BS23 1 UJ. Telephone 01275 884078, Fax: 01275 884168. E-mail: schooladmissionandtransport@n-somerset.gov.uk. The closing date and time for applications is Friday 23rd October 2009 at 4.30 p.m.
