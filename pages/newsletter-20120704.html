French Day

An exciting time was had by all. The day began with Years 3 and 4 excelling at a bilingual book challenge. They were rewarded at break with pains au chocolat and hot chocolate. Everyone had the opportunity to try some French cuisine provided by Miss Schroeder. In the afternoon KS2 had fun making Eiffel Towers from straws while KS1 were entertained by traditional stories told entirely in French. Half way through the afternoon KS2 had time with the storyteller while KS1 had their pains au chocolat and hot chocolate followed by singing French songs.

Year 6 Spirit Of Wells

Year 6 took part in the Spirit of Wells Leavers Day last Wednesday at Wells Cathedral. They enjoyed a range of activities including mime, art, African drumming, team games and storytelling. At the end of the afternoon all of the schools assembled for a reflective service of prayer, singing, mime and a flag parade. Thank you very much to St. Mary’s & St. John’s church for their kind donation of £300 which enabled us to have such a fantastic day!

Head Lice

We have an outbreak of head lice in school again. Please check your child’s head regularly and treat as soon as you spot any unwelcome guests. The advice of the School Nursing team is to always wet comb your child’s hair every three days using conditioner. The Department of Health have a leaflet to download search for Head Lice DH. Another website for information is www.headlice.co.uk.
