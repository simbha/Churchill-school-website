Newsletter - 26th April 2011

A short but very busy week!

Wednesday 27th April

Royal “Street Party” at school in school uniform. Please bring in:-

  • a hat to wear for tea party, there will be a prize for the best hat in each class;
  • an item for the Village Fair tombola – Sunday 1st May. Thank you in advance from the village community.

Thursday 28th April

PTA Royal Disco – KS 1 17:00 and KS 2 18:15.

May Fair – Sunday 1st May

This is a real community event starting with an outdoor church service at 11:00 and stalls and events all day long including wheelbarrow racing, bar, tea tent, “chill out” zone and a pig roast and live music in the evening, all at the Churchill Recreation Field (near the doctor’s surgery). Maypole dancing and singing for our children will be at 13:00.

We have 35 children currently taking part. Please print out and sign the slip below if your child will be taking part and you haven’t previously filled in a slip, and please return by Wednesday 27th April.

And now the rest

There is a “bring your own” picnic on the green (situated on the Broadoak estate), on Friday 29th April from 13:30 and is open to all.

Just to remind you about the PTA “Pamper Night” on 19th May. £3 entrance fee to include a glass of wine and then £5 per treatment after that. If anyone can offer their “beauty” services please contact Katy Pearce on 853094.
