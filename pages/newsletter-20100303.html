Maths Teaching

Maths teaching is always changing, so attached to our Newsletter is an outline of how we teach division, addition, multiplication and subtraction. The methods start with the simplest first and children build up to the more complex strategies. They then use the strategy that they find the easiest. If you are interested in a parents Maths evening, please let your child’s class teacher know.
