Bristol Zoo Trip - YR

Thankfully the weather held off for our very enjoyable visit to the zoo last Thursday. We all had a wonderful time, with the highlights being seeing the lion cubs play-fighting and meeting the baby T-Rex! The children also learned about conservation and were able to look closely at banned animal skins and goods that some people try to bring back to our country. It was sad to see. The children were impeccably behaved and polite, so much so, that a number of Zoo staff commented and thanked them! Well done YR! A big thank you to the lovely mums who helped on the day. It is much appreciated.
