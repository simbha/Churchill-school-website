Churchill Kids' Club

Who are we?

We are an Ofsted registered, non profit making organisation which provides before and after school care to children from Churchill Primary School. Our Ofsted ID is EY376403. Sessions are led by qualified staff with a parents’ committee in charge of organisation. We take up to 16 children per session.

Play workers

Valerie Pain and Sharon Schroeder both work at the school during the day and Anna Lee works at Churchill Pre-school during the day. Sylvia Pain often comes into school to do craft activities with the children acts as cover.

Our hours

7.45-8.45am and 3.30 to 5.45pm. Children may also come on to us after finishing any of the after school clubs.

Prices

Family registration is £12. Single morning sessions are £3.20 per child with a discount for a full week, afternoon ones are £4.30 up to 4.30pm and £6.90 up to 5.45pm with reductions for subsequent children from the same family. We ask for 3 days notice of cancellation or we will charge 50% of the fee.

Where are we based?

Mornings in the hall and in the afternoons either in Key Stage 1 or in the playground.

Games and Play

In the mornings the sessions are based in the main hall and involve arts and crafts, board or card games or just racing about being active! In the afternoon we try to let the children play outside as much as possible to let off steam after the school day. If we are inside we offer crafts, games, reading, homework time.
