Youth Speaks

Last night, two teams from Year 6 took part in the "Youth Speaks" competition, run by the Rotary Club of Wrington Vale. The first team, Georgina Savage, Jasper Badger and Ollie Callow spoke on the subject of "5 a day - is it worth it?" reminding the audience of just how important it is to eat a healthy diet. The second team, Dulcie Davies, Isla Hurst and Jack Slingsby, informed the audience of the dangers of too much exposure to the sun in their talk "The Sun Friend or Foe". Both teams are to be congratulated on their excellent performances and the "Hearty Eaters" have been invited to take part in the regional finals next Spring - well done to all!
