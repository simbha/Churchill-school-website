Churchill Kid's Club

We are an Ofsted registered play scheme providing before and after school care for children at Churchill Primary school from 07:45 in the mornings until 17:45 in the evenings. Children can also come into the club following another after school club. A yearly registration of £14 is required and then you may use the club as often as you like. Childcare funding may be available for you, either via tax credits or via salary sacrifice schemes. We currently have spaces at all sessions. For more details contact the Manager, Valerie Pain, on 07756 355628 or pick up a leaflet in the main school lobby.

An important reminder to all users that Kid's Club AGM is on Wednesday 21st September at 19:00 in the School hall. Find out what the club does and how it is run. New committee members (this means you!) are needed so that the club can continue running.
