Newsletter - 20th July 2011

Summer fair

A huge thank you to everyone who came and supported the fair on Saturday. There was a really happy atmosphere with lots of smiles and laughter throughout the blustery afternoon. Around £2,000 was raised to go towards our new outside play area. Many thanks to the stalwart group of helpers who started work on Friday night putting up the tent and finished on Sunday afternoon washing down the BBQ.
