Mufti Day

Friday, 7th December is Mufti day and in exchange for an item for the tombola or cakes you don’t have to wear school uniform. Mufti is a day for organisations that usually wear uniforms and is a ‘dressing down’ day. Did you know - the word has been used by the British army since 1816 and is thought to derive from the vaguely Eastern style dressing gowns and tasseled caps worn by off-duty officers in the early 19th century?
