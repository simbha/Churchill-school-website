Tree Day 2012

‘Our forests are a gift from our ancestors which must be passed on to our children’.

Last Friday, all dressed in green with wellies and waterproofs, we headed to our local green to join Reverend Kate and the Chairman of North Somerset Council. Together we planted a selection of trees including Rowans, Larches and Oaks, reflecting the name of the streets surrounding our school. During the rest of the day we investigated our native trees and thought some more about the messages from our earlier service, led by Mrs. Johns.
