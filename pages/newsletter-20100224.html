Easter Family Quiz

In response to requests for a children’s quiz event we will be holding a family quiz following school on Tuesday 23rd March. Teams are to be of no more than 6 and must include at least one adult in each team entered. The cost will be £2 per team. There will be a variety of quiz rounds. Light refreshments and hot dogs will also be available to purchase. We anticipate that the quiz should be finished by around 5p.m.
