Newsletter - 15th December 2010

Happy Christmas to our school community. We hope you enjoy the holidays with the children and look forward to seeing everyone back on Tuesday 4th January 2011.

Carol service tonight

Tonight, Wednesday 15th December, is our traditional Carol service at St. John’s Church (next to the Community School). The children have been learning the words to Carols and the orchestra, choir and Year 3 recorder group have been practicing hard. We hope you will join us. Church doors open at 18:15 – dress warmly!

If you can’t join us tonight why not come and sing “around the Christmas tree” on Friday 17th December. The PTA will be giving everyone a “treat” – mulled wine and mince pies. Please join them in the library at 14:15 and then come and sing your heart out.

Christmas lunch

The Christmas lunch was a huge success. Over 300 people shared a beautifully cooked meal. Thank you to everyone who was involved, especially Mrs. Beadle, Ms. Schroeder and Mrs. Brocksom.

The Christmas pudding we made on “Stir Up Monday” was delicious. Well done children!

Win a bike for Christmas

Bike raffle tickets will be on sale each day before school. If you aren’t in school you could send £1 per ticket in an envelope to your child’s class teacher.
