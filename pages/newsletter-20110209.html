Parents Working Party

The Coffee Bite for this week is…

How does my child get into a school run club?

The forms for the clubs come out at the start of the year for all parents to book their children on. If the club is oversubscribed they will be offered a place on a waiting list to join the following term. If you missed the sign up for a club and want to join midway through the year you should contact the teacher concerned.

Some of the clubs available throughout the year are Kitchen, art, ball, choir, chess, Maypole, puzzle, orchestra, netball, running and gardening.
