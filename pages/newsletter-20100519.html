Newsletter - 19th May 2010

Staff, parents and pupils frequently take part in fund-raising events, but Mrs Holdaway (our LSA in Year 1) needs a special mention. Last Saturday Mrs. Holdaway completed the Moonwalk "The finishing line at 9.00am came as a welcome sight, having left Hyde Park at midnight. Watching the sunrise over the Thames feeling the heat of the sun appearing from the dark was amazing. 26.2 Miles didn't seem a long way to begin with, but boy it was! Feathers and laughter got us through the hours, but remembering why we were walking and who it would help. - "Breast Cancer " sustained us. If you wish to donate please drop into the office or see Mrs. Holdaway.
