Reception Visit To Sealife Centre

On Tuesday Reception enjoyed a trip to the Seaquarium in Weston. The children each got to hold a mussel and stingray eggs as well as touch a very slimy anemone! We also saw some sharks and puffer fish. After lunch we went on the beach for a sandcastle competition and some beach races. A good day all day.

Round Table – Penguins

Thank you to Mary and Ellen in Year 2 for their penguin table. The large penguin was called Percy and there were 33 penguins in the jar. Well done to the winners Hannah West (R) and Sophie Smith (Y5).

National Chip Day

Did you know potato chips were invented in 1853 when an angry customer sent his chunky chips back to the chef. The chef, also angry, sent back thin potato slices we now enjoy as crisps! Thank you to Ms. Schroeder and Mrs. Churchill our ‘calm’ chefs!
