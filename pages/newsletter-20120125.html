The Giving Machine: not just for Christmas

You can generate free cash donations for the school every time you shop online via The Giving Machine. More than 100 retailers subscribe to this, including Tesco, Asda, Next, John Lewis, PC World, Toys R Us, Vodafone, eBay and Amazon and it will not cost you anything extra - the online shop pays a donation to the school on your behalf. Just go to www.TheGivingMachine.co.uk, join as a shopper/giver and choose to support Churchill Primary School, or call into the school office where they are offering the service for you.
