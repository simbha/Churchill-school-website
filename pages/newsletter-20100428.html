Youth Speaks

We are very proud to announce that on 22nd April we went to the Kings of Wessex School to take part in the Youth Speaks District Final 2010. We really enjoyed it. Georgina introduced our speech about why we should eat 5 fruit and vegetables a day. Jasper wittily spoke to the large audience about the subject. Oliver summed up Jasper’s speech and told the audience he had “been converted”! Six acts performed and after a long wait they announced the winners. When they called our name (after the runners up) we were so shocked and excited. We really couldn’t believe it. Thank you Mrs Penfold and everybody else that supported us.

Written by Jasper, Oliver and Georgina.

Fund-Raising

May 7th is National Day of “No Sox”. This ties in with our “generosity” theme for Service. We are thinking about children at Woodford Primary School in South Africa who have no “sox” to wear and we invite children to come to school with no socks but shoes/flip flops/trainers etc and to make a donation towards the children at Woodford Primary to enable them to buy socks as they go into their winter season. Thank you in advance for your support.

National Teachers Day

National Teachers Day is May 4th and it is an opportunity for us all to thank the hard working teachers in our school. Our dedicated team put in many hours behind the scenes, preparing lessons, marking and assessing children’s work. They make our school a very special place to be, so to all of them “THANK YOU”.
