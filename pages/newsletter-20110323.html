Parents working party

Coffee and cake bite this week is.... Annual Reports to Parents:-

Staff are always reviewing their work at Churchill Primary to offer you the best education for your child. This year your child's report will be shorter and the time to talk to your child's teacher longer - 15 minutes. A feed back sheet will be given to you at the end of the meeting and the parent working party will collate the information and use this for next years reporting.

In July you will receive a written report showing your child's National Curriculum level. If you have any questions ask a member of the working party for more information.
