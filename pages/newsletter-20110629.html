Fact of the week

Year 2 are learning about the Great Fire of London and at Churchill Primary School in Victorian times the children learnt how to remember the dates of the fire by repeating the following ditty:

"In sixteen hundred and sixty six, London burned like rotten sticks".

Year 3's trip to the Big Pit

Year 3 had a fantastic trip to the Big Pit in Wales. We learned how children as young as 6 worked to hold the doors open in the tunnels. If they dropped their candles they would have to sit in the pitch darkness by themselves. Sometimes rats crawled over their feet and ate their bread if it wasn't in a tin box. Ugh!

By Daniel Long.
