School Meals

Our school has its own kitchen and meals are available under the arrangements made by North Somerset Council. The charge for the term running from 4th September 2012 to 25th October 2012 is £76.00. This may either be paid in advance or paid weekly by bringing £10.00 in a named envelope on Monday Mornings. Cheques should be made out to "North Somerset Council". Please contact the office to let us know of any alterations to a child’s normal pattern of meals.

Children may have a school meal or bring a packed lunch. For those bringing packed lunch, we would ask that no pressurised cans of fizzy drink and no glass containers or bottles are included.
