Head lice

After speaking with the School Nursing Team their advice in the prevention of head lice is to always wet comb your child’s hair every three days using conditioner. The Department of Health have a leaflet to download (PDF, 106K). Another website for information is www.headlice.co.uk.

Hat day

On Friday, 20th January we will be celebrating Hat Day. On that day we would like all the children (and grown ups) to wear a hat of their choice. In addition we are inviting all children to design a special hat. It could be a hat to suit their personality or hobbies. It could be a ‘mad’ hat or a beautiful hat. Get creative with your designs and have fun. Bring in your designs to your class teacher by Thursday, 19th January. On Friday, 20th we will have a special Hat Day service when the children can wear any hat they choose from home and the winning designs will receive prizes.

MEND

A healthy living course aimed at 7 - 13 year olds above a healthy weight is being run from 23rd January to 28th March at Banwell School and 16th April to 18th June at Churchill Sports Centre. This is a free 10 week programme for children and parents to get fit and healthy for free. Funded by North Somerset Council, for more information telephone 0800 300263 or email info@mendcentral.org.

The Giving Machine: not just for Christmas

You can generate free cash donations for the school every time you shop online via The Giving Machine. More than 100 retailers subscribe to this, including Tesco, Asda, Next, John Lewis, PC World, Toys R Us, Vodafone, eBay and Amazon and it will not cost you anything extra - the online shop pays a donation to the school on your behalf. Just go to www.TheGivingMachine.co.uk, join as a shopper/giver and choose to support Churchill Primary School, or call into the school office where they are offering the service for you.
