A Message from Miss Lewis

"I would like to thank you for the gifts you gave me in July - in particular the garden furniture, which in spite of the weather we have had, has been much used and admired; but it was the things you said to me and the sentiments you expressed in writing which moved me the most and will stay with me always - thank you. I have loved working with your children and I hope that I have given as much to them as they have given to me - there really is no better job! Having said that, I am absolutely delighted to have retired!" With my best wishes - Jane Lewis.
