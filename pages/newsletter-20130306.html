Year 3’s Celtic Day

Year 3 enjoyed a wonderful Celtic Day last Thursday, dressing up and finding out about how the ancient Celts used to live. They made Celtic shields and jewellery and our special visitors helped the children build a roundhouse, make string and even grind wheat to make flour. A special thank you to Mr. & Mrs. Hughes, our Celts, and Mrs. Hobson who baked Welsh cakes from dawn ‘til dusk.
