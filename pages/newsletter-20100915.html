Eyes And Teeth

A message from the school nurse, "The School Nursing Team see children in Reception Class to check their vision but do not assess children's vision again. We therefore recommend that parents/carers arrange for their children to be assessed by an optician every 2 years (this is free for children under 16 or under 19 in full-time education). We also recommend that children visit the dentist every 6 months - this is also free for children under 18. For health-related enquiries the School Nursing Team can be contacted on 01275 866724.

Do You Have A Young Child?

Parents of children born between 1st September 2006 and 31st August 2007 are reminded that now is the time to apply for a school place for the 2011-12 school year. You can apply online at www.n-somerset.gov.uk/admissions or request a paper application form from North Somerset School Admissions and Transport Team on 01275 884078. You must apply, even if you have an older child attending the school, as places are not guaranteed for younger brothers and sisters. Please ensure you submit your application by the closing time and date of midnight on 15th January 2011 to be considered in the first round of allocations.
