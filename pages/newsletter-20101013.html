Our Harvest Day

By Elliot Legge And Matthew Darwall - Year 4

In the entrance hall, at the beginning of the day, we felt proud because we saw loads of happy faces and lots of food. When we got into St. Mary's Church we found lots of mums and dads. We felt quite nervous but we enjoyed our performance. We liked showing our work. Our harvest lunch stew smelt good enough to eat. Carrots, potato and beef stew; it tasted delicious. We had all taken turns to peel the vegetables. What a lovely day!
