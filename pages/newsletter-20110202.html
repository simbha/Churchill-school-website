Parents Working Party

The Coffee Bite for this week is... Out of School Events Your children are often invited to take part in out of school events. These are either school trips involving your child's entire class or events held in partnership with other schools or agencies which your child has been selected to take part in. In all cases your permission will be sought and you will be told the date, pickup/collection time, level of supervision, transport requirements (including whether you will need to take/fetch your child) and any food/drink you may need to provide. In all cases you will be told about insurance usually on the initial letter home. You will also be told whether you can or are required to attend the event or need to make arrangements with another parent to look after your child at the event (normally this is the case for after-school activities). Class trips are always fully supervised by the class teachers, LSAs and any volunteer helpers. You will also be given specific information regarding any overnight trips organised by the school. On school trips the children will need to wear their school uniform or PE kit for a sporting event, unless you are advised otherwise. Often trips entail a monetary contribution from parents to cover the cost of transport, entry fees, to museums/attractions, road tolls etc. This is always kept as reasonable as possible but if you have any difficulties in meeting any trip costs please see you child's teacher. If you have any questions do ask your child's teacher or a member of the Parent Working Party - a current list is on the noticeboard.
