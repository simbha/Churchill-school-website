Governors Profile - Claire Hosgood Non-teaching Staff Governor

"I was appointed non-teaching staff governor in October 2005, but I have been working in school since 1999. I am currently the LSA based in Years 4 and 2, but I also have responsibility for children with speech and language difficulties. In this role I work closely with the Speech and Language Therapy department. I became a governor because I wanted to discover how the school worked at a management level.

I am currently on the facilities committee where I have the responsibility for Health and Safety. As a member of the school team I am constantly involved in the life and development of the school. I have 2 children who both went to school here. Prior to having them I worked as a Junior Sister in a Forensic Psychiatric Unit, which involved planning the care of patients and the day to day management of the staff in the unit. Outside school my main interests are friends and family, cooking and reading".

Christmas Competition

Churchill and Langford Parish Council are running a decorated Christmas card competition for children who attend the school, attend Churchill Pre-school or who live in the Parish. The card can be made using any material. It will be collected on Friday, 9th December from school, please put your finished card in an envelope with your name, address and age on the outside and leave in the box outside the office. If your child attends pre-school please take them there for collection. Churchill Post Office will also be collecting. The best designs will be printed in The Tower.
