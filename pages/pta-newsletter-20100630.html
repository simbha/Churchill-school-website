PTA Newsletter - June 2010

PTA Newsletter

The Summer fayre is less than 2 weeks away and we are working hard to try and make this our best summer fayre yet. We thought it might be helpful to give out some reminders of the key dates and other helpful information.

Friday 2nd July
Items for the tombola stall to be bought into school this Friday please.
Friday 9th July
Mufti day in return for cakes and biscuits for the cake stall and also any books or toys (in good condition please) for the toys stall.
Other Donations
We are still looking for donations of unwanted crockery, it can have chips, empty boxes and books and toys. These can be brought in at any time.

Arts & crafts Show

Last years arts and craft event was so popular it's back by demand and we are holding our second arts and craft show at the fayre. All entries need to be taken to school between 3.30 - 6p.m on Friday 9th July. I'm afraid due to time constraints and judging we will not be able to accept entries outside of this time.

5 a side football tournament

At the fayre this year we will be holding a children's 5 a side football tournament. All teams will need to register at 2.15p.m on the school field ready for the tournament to start from 3p.m. England may not be in the world cup but how about showing your soccer skills off in our fun footy tournament! There will be different competitions for the different ages. Teams to be made up of children from the same year. Start getting your teams together now!

Children's school Calendar

Over the past few weeks the children have all been working on their self portraits which will go into our school calendar to be on sale at the fayre. This calendar will run over the school academic year and as well as fantastic self portraits it will hold all the key term dates etc. The calendars will be available at the fayre at the bargain price of £2.50 or if you would like to bulk buy for relatives etc then 5 for £10.

End of term disco

Our final disco of the year will be held on Thursday 15th July. As usual KS1 5-6p.m and KS2 6.15-7.15p.m £2 per child to include free drinks.

Help still needed

Thank you to all those people who have already offered to help in many ways at the fayre but we are still looking for more help so if you haven't already done so we'd really appreciate it if you could offer a small amount of time to help make this a really successful event. Please see your class rep or any member of the PTA.

And finally..

If you have any more budding millionaires out there who've grown their pounds could we please have all monies in asap please. Also, anyone who was unable to take part if you could please return your pound to your class teacher who can mark you as having returned the money. We will let you know how much this endeavour has raised but the children who have taken part have had some fantastic ideas, experiences and really inspiring money making ways - watch out Alan Sugar! Well done to you all.
