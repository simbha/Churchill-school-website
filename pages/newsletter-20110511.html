Mrs. Holdaway Walking London In Her Bra!

Mrs. Holdaway, Year 1 LSA, will be taking part in the 14th Moonwalk in London this weekend, walking 26 miles, in this extraordinary moonlit marathon. Over 15,000 brave walkers will be pounding the streets of London to raise awareness and money for breast cancer. One in eight women in the UK expects to experience some form of breast cancer. Anyone wishing to donate please see Mrs. Holdaway or leave your donations in the office.
