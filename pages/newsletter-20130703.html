Spelling Support Pack

In response to the suggestions made by those parents who joined us on our Spelling and Reading evening back in April, we have put together a small pack of bits and pieces that we hope will help you to support your child’s spelling and reading. The pack includes phonics pronunciations/actions, common spelling rules, paragraphs to encourage children to ask questions and make connections and a set of thought bubbles that you can use with your child to stimulate discussion about what they have read. If you would like a pack please complete and return the slip at the end of the paper version of this newsletter, to the office, by Monday, 8th July 2013.

We have also purchased a number of KS2 Spelling Support booklets that set out the various spelling rules and strategies that your child will meet throughout years 3, 4, 5 and 6. These are available from the office at a small cost of £1.25.
