Fact of the week St. Patrick’s style

"In Ireland on St. Patrick ’s Day people traditionally wear a small bunch of shamrocks on their jackets or caps. Children wear orange, white and green badges symbolising the Irish flag and females wear green ribbons in their hair."

Parking in Larch Close

Some of the residents in Larch Close have brought to our attention the bad parking around the entrance to Larch Close. Please be mindful that you do not obstruct this area and please make sure that the children do not walk on any of the gardens.

According to the AA, children on foot are more likely to be killed in road accidents in Britain than in Austria, Belgium, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany the Netherlands, Spain or Sweden.

Year 1 Blaise Castle museum trip

Year 1 and staff thoroughly enjoyed dressing up as Victorians. All the children were very well behaved and no one had to stand in the corning wearing a dunces hat! They were able to see some very old delicate toys and then investigate for themselves how old fashioned toys worked.

Netball
