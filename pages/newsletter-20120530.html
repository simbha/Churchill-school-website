Village Jubilee 3rd June

Don’t forget this Sunday is the Village Jubilee Party. Worship at 11.00am with the Jubilee Choir and the opening at 12.00 noon. There is a BBQ during the day and a pig roast in the early evening.

Queen Maisie John, King Edward Cockram and their attendants – Angus Bird, Isaac Morris-Shapiro, Stan Hemmings, Libby Wilson, Danielle Mitchell and Lailah Knight will be riding in an old bus from the school to the rec field departing at 11.50am. Any children in red, white and blue will also be welcome to join the bus. Stalls and children’s entertainment with inflatables, children’s disco with DJ Ed at 5.30. Dance to Garry Sharp’s band, Deep River Blue. Make this a day to remember.

I your would like to donate a cake please bring to school on Friday or to the Rec field Saturday or Sunday morning.

If anyone would like to help for an hour on the day either setting up or helping to run a stall please let me know.

Bristol Rovers FC

Bristol Rovers Football Club is launching a NEW Advanced Development Centre based at Churchill Leisure Centre. The Development Centre, run by Elite Development, shall give the Club the opportunity to recruit boys from within the North Somerset catchment area with the aim to provide opportunities for talented players to progress to the Bristol Rovers FC Academy (formerly the Centre of Excellence). To register for the forthcoming trials in July, or for further information please contact Advanced Development Centre Manager Jonathan Henderson by calling 07401 207 200 or by Email: jonny@elitedc.org.
