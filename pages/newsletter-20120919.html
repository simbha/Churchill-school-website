Roald Dahl Day

“I know exactly what words I am wanting to say, but somehow or other they is always getting squiff-squiddled around.” BFG.

It wasn’t just the BFG getting tongue tied in his 30th birthday last Thursday, we all loved experimenting with whizumptious words. From hipscuddles and gunzlebumkum to squiffscuddle and piffle swash — we created oodles of exciting new  vocabulary by joining prefixes and suffixes. Like the BFG we also collected ours dreams in a dream jar and learnt about big dreamers of the past. Some of us also met, drew and described Fleshumpeaters and Bonecrunchers! Matthew in Year 3 said:

“The day was fun and we made dream jars and decorated them with patterns in different colours”.
