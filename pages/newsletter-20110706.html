St. John the Baptist - Picnic

Ben and William in Year 4 wrote:

“Churchill Primary School went for a whole school picnic at Windmill Hill. We were celebrating St. John the Baptist and learnt about how he baptised Jesus. We also learnt about how John the Baptist died. Each class designed and carried their own shield based on his story and Mrs. Smallwood, from St. Johns Church, said the grace. We thought it was an exciting way to learn and celebrate St. John the Baptist Day”.
