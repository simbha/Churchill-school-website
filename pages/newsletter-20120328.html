Register Now For Free School Meals

This is not something that would usually feature in the Newsletter but Free School Meals are very important. So please read on! Many families in the Churchill catchment are entitled to claim Free School Meals, but many don’t. The number of eligible families has risen remarkably since 2008 partly due to the great banking crisis and the ‘credit crunch’. We are sure there are families out there who do probably qualify but who don’t register and this is the purpose of this appeal. If you are in receipt of certain benefits you are likely to qualify and registering for Free School Meals does not affect your other entitlements. We would urge you to do this as soon as possible. Firstly, accessing Free School Meals can save you between £300 - £400 per year. Secondly, if you register it is entirely up to you – and your child/children – if you actually take them, but please still register. Why? Registering for Free School Meals brings the school extra income. We use the Pupil Premium in several ways – to provide extra resources in school such as one-to-one intervention and extra-curricular activities. Registering for Free School Meals is entirely confidential so please telephone North Somerset Council’s School Admissions Department on 01275 884014.
