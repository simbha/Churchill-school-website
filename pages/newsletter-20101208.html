Our visit to the pantomime

Last Friday we went on an exciting trip to the Blakehay Theatre. We watched the pantomime Jack and the Beanstalk. My favourite character was Simple Simon because he was funny and made us shout “Hi, Si, your tree's gone dry!” Then he watered a plant and it grew.

The funniest and loudest part was when the ghosts came as the characters were singing. We all had to shout “Ghost!”.

We are sure that everyone in the school enjoyed it including the staff and especially us.

By Phoebe Samuel and Lucy Slingsby in Year 5.

If there are huge amounts of snow and you think the school may be closed please look on the school website:
