Year 6 Sats 2010

With term 5 rapidly approaching, we thought we would take this opportunity to provide a little more information about the SATs your child will be sitting the week beginning Monday 10th May. We hope to have covered all aspects of the tests but if there should be anything you are unsure about please do not hesitate to talk to one of us.

Preparation

Reading

Writing

Maths Homework (Easter)

Regularly practising and using mental maths, including times tables and the four operations.

Science Homework (Easter)

Though there are no compulsory Science papers this year, we will be assessing the children's scientific knowledge during the week of SATs.

If you child should want to keep their science ticking over this website is really useful: www.compare4kids.co.uk/learn.php

At home - support you can offer

  • Literacy - reading, writing in full sentences with detail & description, questioning why language has been included.
  • Numeracy - times tables, mental maths,
  • Not too much pressure.
  • Relax the weekend before!
  • Get to bed early each night before tests.
  • Please let us know if your children are feeling stressed or have any concerns.

On the Day

  • Please ensure your child arrives promptly in school every morning - lateness cannot be accommodated. Any late children will complete tests away from the rest of the class.
  • Tests will take place in classes 6, the library and studio.
  • Numerous staff will be available.
  • Discussion and warm-up before starting paper.
  • Read through information in paper together.
  • Children will be aware of time available.
  • Some children will work with a reader.
  • All children can have questions read to them in Maths papers.
  • Children can look back over paper - will be encouraged to check their work.
  • In case of illness - can stop and continue later.
  • Cheating will not be tolerated - the QCA guidance states that papers in this instance will be removed from the children and they will score zero on the papers in question.
  • We can scribe over handwriting as necessary.

After the Tests

Results

  • School hopes to get results at beginning of July.
  • Results need to be checked by school.
  • We hope to share results by middle of July - you will receive a written summary for your child.

Secondary School

  • Secondary schools will receive SAT scores and teacher assessments.
