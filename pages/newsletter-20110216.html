Parking

We have had some concerns from the community about the way in which parents are parking when collecting their children including allowing them to get out of cars into the road. Please be aware that the Highway Code states:

Rule No. 242 You MUST NOT leave your vehicle or trailer in a dangerous position or where it causes any unnecessary obstruction of the road.

Rule No. 243 DO NOT stop or park opposite or within 10 metres (32 feet) of a junction, except in an authorised parking space.

Parents Working Party

The Coffee Bite for this week is:

The Maths/Numeracy evening

Mrs. Lloyd hosted a very informative maths/numeracy evening for parents to explain some of the terms and methods that the children are using in school. A booklet has been sent home accompany the evening regarding these methods and if you have any queries please see your class teacher. Some of the Parents Working Party attended and have come away with a better understanding of how maths is being taught in school and how we can help our children at home. There were some very useful books on sale and these can be purchased from the office at a cost of £4 each.

Walk To School

A total of 130 children took part in Walk to School not quite reaching last years numbers of 180. Each child that walked to school had a ticket and this was put into a draw to win bike locks. The winners were Reece Stinchcombe, Year 2, Oliver Hill, Year 4 and Ben Baker, Year 5; congratulations.
