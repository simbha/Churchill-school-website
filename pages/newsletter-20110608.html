Newsletter - 8th June 2011

Welcome back to the "summer" term. We are all looking forward to enjoying our lovely outside areas. Over the holidays we have had some new storage areas installed for outside play equipment. This will enable our lunchtime assistants to offer a wider variety of activities to the children. We are eagerly awaiting the construction of a small amphitheatre and traversing wall (with slide to be purchased by PTA). Details to follow.

Quiz Night

Don't forget this Saturday is the PTA Quiz Night starting at 7.30pm. Teams will Consist of 6 people (or thereabouts) and costs £5 per person to include supper. Why not get a class team together and challenge last times winners?

Walk To School Week

Monday, 13th to Friday, 17th June is Walk to School Week. Please encourage your child to walk, cycle, skate or scoot to school. We will try to beat our previously best record of 92%. To further encourage our healthy start to each day a different year group will be time tabled to play on their bikes, scooters at lunchtime (weather permitting). If you have a bike a helmet will be required. Reception class and Year 1 will be on the Monday, Years 2 and 3 will be on Tuesday, Year 4 on Wednesday, Year 5 Thursday and Year 6 Friday.

Assembly Theme

The theme for the next week is the meaning of Pentecost.

Fact Of The Week

Lucy and Sophie in Year 4 have found out that the moon weighs 81 billion billion tons!

Scooters

If your child brings a scooter to school please remind them it should be "parked" in the cycle bay by the back gate until the end of the school day (except when it is their class's lunchtime scooter day). For everyone's safety we ask that children do not ride scooters in the playground before and after school. Thank you.

International Week Week Beginning 13th June

Children will be learning about differences and similarities of childhood around the world and enjoying drumming, food, puppets, arts and crafts, map work and many other activities.

Summer Fair

If you would like to place an advertisement in our PTA school booklet, which will go out to over 500 houses in early July, please come to the school office for more information. There are plans to include an "Art and Craft Show" section, so get planting now. Remember, if you have any old or unwanted crockery please bring it to the school office. Thank you.

School Choir
