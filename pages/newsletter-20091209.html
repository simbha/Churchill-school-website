Christingle

Every year Christingle services are held in schools and Churches around the country to raise money for 'The Children's Society'. The Children's Society is a leading children's charity committed to making childhood better for all children in the UK The Children's Society works daily to rescue and support disadvantaged children who face violence, neglect and poverty in their daily lives. The 6000 Christingle celebrations around the country play a big part in raising the money needed to provide this support.

'I was living in a car park when The Children's Society found me. Bad stuff happened on the streets, but now I'm not afraid anymore'

Every Year 100,000 children and Young people run away from home. The Children"s Society is here to help. They have specialist project workers around the country helping children like Ben, But they can't do this without your support. Act now. Make childhood better. £10 could provide a homeless child on the streets with basic provisions including warm clothing, toiletries and a blanket.

Churchill Kids Club - before and after school care at Churchill Primary School

We are an Ofsted registered play scheme which operates on every day of the school year from 7.45am to 8.45am in the mornings and 3.30pm to 5.45pm in the evenings. In the mornings our tearn will ensure your child gets to their classroom (KS1) or the playground (KS2) ready for the start of school. In the afternoons children may come directly from their classrooms or from one the school clubs. Initial registration (£12 per family per year) is required but then the club maybe used regularly or on an ad hoc basis (morning sessions are £3.20 per child or £12.80 for the whole week, afternoon sessions start at £4.30 for the first child then £3.20 for subsequent children). For further details please contact Play leader Valerie Pain on 07756 355628 or talk to her during a session or see our notice board in the lobby or pick up a leaflet in the school reception.
