Dogs in the Playground

For Health and Safety reasons, the only dogs which are allowed on the school premises are registered Guide Dogs – please carry all others!

Ticks Alert!

Please be aware that ticks are about! Be sure to check your children regularly for these very small, black insects which burrow into the skin. If you find one, please take your child to the Doctor’s Surgery to have it checked out.
