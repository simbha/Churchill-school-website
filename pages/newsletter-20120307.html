Year 6 Steam Trip

Year 6 went to the Steam Museum in Swindon on Tuesday. Daniel and Benedict said:

“We extremely enjoyed the trip because we learnt how an evacuee in WW2 would feel in their every day life and their emotions. Our favourite part was when the siren went off and we had to walk into the air-raid shelter. It was an amazing experience”.

Music Festival

Charles and Ellen-mai from Year 5 said:

“A BIG thank you to Mrs Barrett and Miss Mullins for letting us go and take part in the music festival, we really enjoyed singing and hearing all about the aliens travelling to Proxima. We learnt a lot about Proxima from this and the narration. Thank you also for all the mums and dads that come to watch us”.

The Giving Machine—Please remember to click

You can generate free cash donations for the school every time you shop online via The Giving Machine. More than 100 retailers subscribe to this, including Tesco, Asda, Next, John Lewis, PC World, Toys R Us, Vodafone, eBay and Amazon and it will not cost you anything extra - the online shop pays a donation to the school on your behalf. Just go to www.TheGivingMachine.co.uk, join as a shopper/giver and choose to support Churchill Primary School, or call into the school office where they are offering the service for you.
