Fact of the week

Provided this week by Year 4:

“The Guinness Book of Records holder for the largest Easter egg ever made is the Belgian chocolate maker Guylian who made the chocolate egg with at least 50,000 bars for the city of St. Niklaas. The egg measured 8.32 metres high. Twenty-six craftsman worked altogether 525 hours to build the egg. They needed 1950kg of chocolate.
