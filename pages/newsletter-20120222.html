Governors Profile – Hayley West, Parent Governor

I was appointed as a parent governor in December 2011. I have two children at Churchill Primary and can be found most mornings and afternoons in the school playground.

I have recently been asked to join the personnel committee. As a parent I have always tried to involve myself with school activities and hope to be more involved as a parent governor.

I work part-time as a Paediatric Nursing Sister at the Bristol Royal Hospital for Children, I have had the pleasure of caring for children and their families for many years and hope to bring some of these experiences to the role of school governor.

I enjoy many outdoor pursuits, gardening, reading and going to the gym.

School Council Report

The school council have been working on a number of projects. They have been encouraging children not to play fight in the playground as this can lead to unpleasantness. They reported at the last meeting that there is now very little “play fighting” at lunchtimes. They have also been getting pupils to respect the toilets as you would any other room! Flushing every time after use, washing hands etc. Their next project is to organise pictures for the toilet walls.
