Newsletter - 3rd October 2012

Thank you to the huge numbers of people who supported our harvest fundraising event. So many people helped to make the day successful from Libby and John Cook opening the church at 8.30am so that we could go in and arrange our rustic flowers which had been picked by willing hands the night before — to Mrs. Lloyd who delivered the goods to the hospice on Friday after school. Over £400 was raised. Thank you to Mrs. Williams for running the raffle of two beautiful boxes of fruit & veg that had been donated. The staff, parents, governors and helpers did a fantastic job to ensure everyone was fed and enjoyed Miss Schroeder’s cooking at its best.

Festive Fun Run

Have you been inspired by the fantastic summer of sport? Do you want to get fit and lose some weight before the Christmas season? Then sign up for our Festive Fun Run! A small group of parents and governors are organising a fun run and 5k run on the morning of Saturday 8th December and will be open to parents, staff and children young and old. Look out for more information over the next couple of weeks, including registration forms and posters on the noticeboards. In the meantime, put the date in your diaries and start training! Every week we will print a training tip from parents or staff.
