Governors profile – Nick Hobson, Parent Governor

I was appointed as a parent governor in September 2009 when my eldest son started at the school. We have lived in the area long enough to remember watching the new school being built, my wife is a Deputy Head Teacher at another North Somerset school which has helped with the transition to being a governor as I was already familiar with some of the terminology and abbreviations used in education.

I work for a large logistics company which involves a lot of driving but does also offer some flexibility which has enabled me to be involved at Churchill during the school day, mainly in recruitment as I am currently on the Personnel Committee.

When I get the time I enjoy mountain biking with some of the other dads at the school – with so many great trails on the doorstop why not come along and join us!

The Giving Machine – Shop online & raise funds for our school

Just go to www.TheGivingMachine.co.uk join as a shopper/giver and choose to support Churchill Primary School.
