Harvest Time

What an amazing day, the church services were uplifting - the children's enthusiasm was wonderful. The lunch was delicious and the gifts of food you donated numerous! The raffle at the end of the day raised £50. The chef at Weston Hospice was delighted with the food gifts and promised to cook the marrows and garlic grown by the children with great care.
