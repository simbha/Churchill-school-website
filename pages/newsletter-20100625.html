Dyslexia Awareness Course

On Thursday 30th September from 10.00 a.m. – 12.00 p.m. at 14 Whiteladies Road, Clifton, Bristol, there will be the opportunity to attend a Dyslexia Awareness Course covering “What is Dyslexia?” with tips and strategies for Reading and Spelling. This is designed to help parents and Teaching Assistants to understand and help children meet their potential. Cost: £20 per person including refreshments.

Spaces are limited so please call 0117 9239166 or e-mail bristol@dyslexiaaction.org.uk.
