First Aid In School

Please be assured that if your child has an accident in school a number of our staff are paediatric first aid trained and if it is considered a significant injury you will be contacted. In the event of a bumped head or other body parts the child is given a sticker to wear and a note to go home to parents. Class teachers are informed and a child will be observed during the course of the day. However when children are out of sight of a member of staff sometimes they take off their sticker! So please check for a ‘note’ in your child’s bag.

Sunny Spain Comes To Churchill

In the midst of this cold snap, music and dance from Spain has been lifting our spirits. In the first of a series of assemblies on World Music, pianist and educationalist Sue Curtis introduced us to the colourful music and dance of Spain. She demonstrated how to use rhythms and harmonies to make the piano sound Spanish before playing a piece by Spanish composer Isaac Albeniz.

Sue, who has a degree in Music and English from Birmingham University, a piano teaching qualification from the Royal College of Music and a Postgraduate Certificate in Education from Oxford University, offers piano tuition to children and adults, beginners to advanced and can be contacted on 01761 462485 or 0796 206 3883 or sue@citruscreative.co.uk.
