Newsletter - 30th June 2010

The recent Swimming Gala, held at Churchill Sports Centre, was a huge success and we are very happy to announce that Churchill Primary School came first in the large schools category with 72 points. The other results were: Winscombe second with 65 points, Wrington third with 48 points and Banwell fourth with 31 points. Very well done to all those children who took part - we are proud of you!

What a wonderful weekend - lets hope the weather will be as good for our summer fair on 10th July.

Out and About

Next week Year 4 will be spending the day following the course of the River Parrot; I'm looking forward to their report.

Year 3 visited the Big Pit in Blaenavon Pontypool. Here is a short report from Harry Ford:-

"One day I went to school really excited about going to a place in Wales called the Big Pit...

When we got out of the cage we walked through some doors called Pit Pony Doors because the miners used to open them for the Pit Ponies. Then we walked on, it was interesting because there were dates and signatures on the wall and there were two metal bars that rang a bell. I thought it was fun."
