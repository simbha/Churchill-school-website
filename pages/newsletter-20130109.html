Newsletter - 9th January 2013

Happy New Year!

From all the staff thank you for our cards and Christmas gifts, they were much appreciated. My chocolate and raspberry biscuits were delightful dipped in coffee over the holidays. Although this is a short term we have plenty of exciting activities planned for the children to enjoy and learn from.

Christingle Service

A total of £166.47 for the Children’s Society was collected in the candles that were sent home. Thank you to Kate Sax for leading our service and Mrs. Smallwood for organising all the oranges, sweets and candles for the children.

Carol Service At St. John’s Church

It was a delightful evening for parents and children. Year 6 led the service with great professionalism. Thank you to Miss Mullins for working with staff to create a chime bar medley.

Carols Around The Tree

Thank you to all the parents who joined us ‘around the tree’ which was kindly donated by Brockley flower stall. This is a friendly family run stall that have a beautiful selection of spring bulbs at the moment. The tea and coffee organised after the event by the PTA were much appreciated. We are so lucky to have yet another hard working team.

Somerset Soccer Schools are offering free soccer taster sessions for boys and girls from Reception up to Year 6.
