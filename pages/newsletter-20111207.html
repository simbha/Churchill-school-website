Newsletter - 7th December 2011

Christmas Fayre

What a fantastic Christmas Fayre! The staff, children and PTA worked very hard to prepare the school for this annual event, which started with the arrival of Father Christmas in a vintage car!

The school corridors buzzed with the usual comings and goings and over £2000 was made with £1200 being made on the Promise Auction.

Thank you to everyone who supported us.

How many balloons in a car?

Churchill Kids Club would like to say a big “Thank you!” to everyone who tried to guess how many balloons were in a car, the answer was 47. Also thank you to William Maitland-Round for his help on the day and the Grist family for the use of their car.

Aladdin

We all had a great time, the acting and singing by the students of Weston College was fantastic. A bit thank you to the PTA for their continued support of this event.

John Abdy

One of the previous vicars of our Parish has sadly died after an illness. His funeral will be taking place on Monday, 12th December, 11:00 at Burrington Church. John worked hard to ensure the Christian ethos of our school was strong in all that we do, he will be sadly missed.

Youthful Spirit Christmas Concert

Adult tickets are £6, children £3. For more information please contact Alison on 853316.
