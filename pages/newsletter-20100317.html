The Village Fund

The Village Fund Committee are holding their AGM tonight (17th March) at 8 p.m. at The Langford Inn. Please come along and join them for a glass of wine and give them your views. Over the last 20 years they have raised and distributed many thousands of pounds to a number of local causes.

In Case You Have Forgotten…
