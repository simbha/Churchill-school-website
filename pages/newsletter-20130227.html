Ofsted Interventions

Since our Ofsted in October we have all be working hard to improve the areas they notified as “requiring improvement”. We are still waiting for a visit from Her Majesty’s Inspector (HMI) who was due 4-6 weeks after the inspection!

Mrs. Lloyd has seen improvements in the planning and teaching of maths. We have updated our assessment system so that we ensure pupils are continually making progress and if not we are taking action to support their learning. The staff team are feeling very positive about our progress and look forward to the HMI visit to validate our work.
