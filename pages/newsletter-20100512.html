Reminder - Inset Days

We have three Inset days attached to the Whitsun Half Term so the last day of School will be Friday 28th May and the children will return to school on Thursday June 10th. Inspirational

Members of the after school Kitchen Club spent the day at Cannington College building the garden they had designed. A huge "thank you" to the parents who transported and helped and Mr Anderson and Mrs Churchill for their sterling work. The children's garden was "Best in Show" - look at the website soon to see the project.

Middle Combe Nursery were very generous with their plants, so next time you need some good quality plants for your garden, do go and visit them.
