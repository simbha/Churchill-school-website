Best Big, Fat, Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

We enjoyed our chocolate chip cookies so much we thought we would share the recipe with you:

Ingredients

  • 250g plain flour
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 170g melted butter
  • 200g dark brown soft sugar
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 1tbls vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 325g chocolate chips

Method

Preheat oven to 170ºC/Gas mark 3. Grease baking trays or line with parchment. Sift together flour, bicarb & salt; set aside. In a medium bowl, cream together melted butter, brown sugar & caster sugar until well blended. Beat in the vanilla, egg and egg yolk until light and creamy. Mix in the sifted ingredients until just blended. Stir in the chocolate chips by hand. Drop 4 tbls cookie dough onto the prepared baking trays. For smaller cookies drop 1 rounded tbls and adjust baking time as necessary. Do not flatten the dough. Cookies should be about 8cm apart.
