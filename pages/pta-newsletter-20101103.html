Christmas Hamper

This year we will be having a prize to the best decorated class hamper box. Each class will decorate their class box in school. We will be putting up lists for items required outside each of the classrooms and we'd be grateful if you could sign up to donating one of the items from your child's class hamper list.

Promise Auction

Every year the promise auction helps raise extra funds at the Christmas Fayre and we can guarantee some fantastic promises. However we need your help to make this a success. We will be asking for donations of promises - anything from baking a special cake, babysitting etc to a weekend break away, the sky's the limit! If you know of any local companies or businesses that would also donate a promise then that would be great too. See your class rep, any member of the PTA or Liz/Jackie in the school office if you can help.
