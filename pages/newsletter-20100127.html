Finally The Football Report

Whilst I was at a school football tournament that was held at Wyvern School, we played 4 matches; after we played the last match I was approached by one of the coaches there. He said "Hey mate you're an excellent player and would you like to join the North Somerset football team". When he asked me I replied "yes!" with excitement and I was really pleased with myself. A couple of weeks later they emailed with dates of the training session to meet all the other players. I went along and I was the only year 5, all the rest were year 6. He said to me "Don't be intimidated by the older ones, just play your normal game". I really enjoyed myself and I am waiting for the next training session and match.

By Elliott Shipway
