Mrs. Williams

After ‘many years’ at Churchill Primary School Mrs. Williams will be retiring at the end of this year. She has organised maths, geography, history and music during her time with us. Her classes have benefitted from her further teaching and many interests especially in promoting care of our environment. We will be dedicating a T@3 to her later in the year.

St. George’s Day Parade

Mark Taylor in Year 5 wrote a report on St. George’s Day Parade. He said:

“On Sunday the 22nd a Scout, Beaver, Adventurer and Cub Parade was held for St. George’s day. Me and my brothers Jack and Kyle were there. Kyle carried his group’s flag. Half of the Axe district was there. We started at Weston beach, most of the time it was raining and we walked to a park with a band stand, we had 3 live bands. We sang 3 songs and the national anthem. About 20 awards were given out. We all had fun”.
