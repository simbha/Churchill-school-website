Feel you could help?

The Community School have joined forces with us in encouraging children to knit blankets to send to Woodford Primary School in South Africa. If you have any remnants of wool you would like to donate please pop them into the office. If you would like to help our children knit or would like to form a parents “knitting” group please come and let us know.

The Giving Machine

It is still not too late to register with TheGivingMachine. This is a website where you can generate cash donations to the school every time you shop online using major stores including Next, Marks & Spencer and Amazon. Registering is easy and it won’t cost you anything extra. Go to:- www.TheGivingMachine.co.uk
