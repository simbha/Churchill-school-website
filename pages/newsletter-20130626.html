Zakhele Concert: Tuesday 2nd July, 7.00pm, Churchill Sports Centre

Zakhele are a highly talented and vibrant group of musicians from the poorest parts of South Africa, near the Swaziland border. They visit Churchill Academy on 1st & 2nd July to spend time with students and share their culture, music and life experiences. Zakhele love to sing and dance and they would like to share their talent with you at a Charity Concert on Tuesday 2nd July at 7pm. Please come along and support the local charity supporting Zakhele (the Za Foundation) and see them working in partnership with Churchill instrumentalists, dancers and the Gospel Choir. You should not miss this astonishing spectacle of creativity. Tickets: Adults £4.00; Under 18s £2.00 from www.positickets.co.uk.

The Giving Machine – Shop Online & Raise Funds For Our School

You can generate free cash donations for the school every time you shop online via The Giving Machine. More than 400 retailers subscribe to this, including Amazon, M&S, eBay, Tesco, Sainsbury’s, Next, John Lewis and Argos. It will not cost you anything extra - the online shop pays a donation to the school on your behalf. At the moment we only have 2 active givers but have still received a quarterly payment of £30…..imagine how much more we could raise if more people joined! Just go to www.TheGivingMachine.co.uk to join as a shopper/giver and search for “Churchill Primary School PTA”. Thank you.
