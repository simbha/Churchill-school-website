Boomfest

Boomfest is an exciting new festival for children, young people and families in North Somerset run by boomsatsuma, a series of youth arts co-ops run entirely by young people. They are holding a festival in Clarence Park from 19th – 21st August and showcasing talented children in the area. There will be workshops and performances through dance, live music, theatre, film skateboarding to name a few. For more details look at their website www.boomsatsuma.com.

Parents working party

Coffee bite, this week’s piece is smaller so you will only need one biscuit and a small mug full:

Your child may come home from school telling you they have had Mrs X working with them today. Twice a week Classes 1-6 are split into 2 groups for Maths and English and work in different rooms. Groups are led by either the class teacher or the PPA teacher both of whom know your children well. This gives the teachers an opportunity to work more closely with the children and target individual needs more easily than in a class of 30. This way of working has been very beneficial for all children and is know as “Class split”
