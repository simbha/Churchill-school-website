Arts Week

To celebrate our Artsmark Gold award, over the next seven days we are having an Arts Week with a golden theme: "A pot of gold at the end of the rainbow". Miss Ingram is leading dance workshops throughout the school and we are hoping our Dream Catchers, organised by Mrs Lockyer, will let the children's golden dreams pass through. Ms Joskey has selected some fitting music and songs and we will be exploring how gold has inspired contemporary artists. Thank you to Mr and Mrs Marlow and all the parents who have helped to make this such a success.

Thank you also to Jasper Badger and Eleanor Wright for representing the school so magnificently on Monday at the Artsmark Award Ceremony in Cheltenham.
