Swimming - Urgent Request For Help

We are very sorry to see the departure of Mrs Caulfield from our team of swimming staff. Over the past 3 years she has taken responsibility for one of our swimming groups and her help will be sorely missed.

If you are an excellent swimmer, or swimming teacher, and feel able to help us regularly on a Tuesday afternoon (from 1.30pm) with Y3/4 swimming we would be delighted to hear from you. You will need to undertake a North Somerset CRB check and the office has the necessary paperwork. For safety reasons you are not required to enter the pool with the children.
