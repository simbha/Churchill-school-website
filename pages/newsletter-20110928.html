The Governors

The Governing Body works hard to support staff and pupils in making our school a great place to learn, work and play. So that you may get to know your Governors better we are going to feature each one in the newsletter over the next few weeks.

This week is Sally Furniss Chair of the Governors and LEA Governor:

I have served as a Governor here for over 20 years.

My own boys were educated here right through from Reception to 6th Form at the Community School. One is a doctor and the other an accountant and I am sure that their success has been built on the foundations of their time at Churchill Primary.

I am currently serving as Chair of the Governing Body, and am part of the Management Committee. I also have special interest in the teaching of maths.

My interest in science, food and nutrition led me to choose Dietetics as a profession. I work at Weston General Hospital, where I manage a team of ten Dieticians, and in my spare time love gardening, running and all my menagerie of animals.

Why am I a Governor? Because of the children and because it is a wonderful school. I hope I can play a small part in its successful future development.
