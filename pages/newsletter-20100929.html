Newsletter - 29th September 2010

This has been a delightful week with children enjoying learning about harvest in their own community and across the world. Children have picked blackberries, made apple cake and harvest loaf, gathered the vegetables from the school garden, prepared vegetables for harvest stew and learnt songs and poems. A truly special time of year.
