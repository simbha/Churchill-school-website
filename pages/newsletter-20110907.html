Newsletter - 7th September 2011

Welcome to a new year at Churchill Primary! We are delighted that you have trusted us with your child and we will strive to ensure that they have a happy and fulfilling year in which they will reach their full potential.

School rules and Home School agreement

All the children have agreed and “signed up” to our school rules and we hope you will remind and encourage your child to follow them:-

Our School Rules

  • I will treat everyone as I would like them to treat me.
  • I will follow instructions first time.
  • I will move sensibly and safely around the school and always walk when inside.
  • I will try to do my best and let others do the same.
  • This is my world, I will look after everything in and around it.

To get the most from school life we like to work in partnership, so we are sending our “Home School” Agreement for you to discuss with your child. Please sign the slip and return by Monday, 12th September; keep the agreement for reference. This agreement will also be on the school website.

A few housekeeping reminders

  • Buggies need to stay in the playground; we have had all the carpets cleaned!
  • Healthy breaks are preferred e.g. fruit and veg – but the choice is yours.
  • Please collect your children from the playground to avoid congestion in the corridors.
  • If you have changed your contact details please let us know immediately.
  • If you child’s medical needs change please notify us in writing.

We try our best to look after your child so tell us straight away if you are concerned so we can put your mind at rest. We have 200 children who will at times squabble, tussle and tease each other. Part of school life is for children to learn strategies for managing this. We have a very good staff team here to support you, your children and each other.
