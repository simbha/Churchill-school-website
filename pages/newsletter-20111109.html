Governors profile – Lesley Hegarty LEA Governor

I was appointed as a Parent Governor in 2001 when my children, Helen and Patrick, were at the school. As Helen has now left Churchill Academy and Patrick is in Year 11 my role has transferred to that of Local Authority Governor. I still remain very committed to the school and in particular its role within the local community, and am the governor with a special interest in Literacy.

During my time as governor I have been Chair of the Personnel Committee, Vice Chair of the Governing Body and am now Chair of the Curriculum Committee.

I love all sports (especially tennis), sing in a gospel choir, am a member of the Churchill and Langford Village Fund Committee and enjoy the company of family and friends.

I have a degree in Modern Languages, spent time in the Royal Navy, was a stockbroker in the City and am now a garden designer.
