Newsletter - 12th September 2012

Autumn seems to be with us, so time to plan Harvest.

Harvest lunch letters have already been sent out, so we look forward to seeing as many parents as possible on Thursday, 27th September. We will be inviting the children to bring in produce on 27th. The highlight of our Harvest celebration will be our church service, we welcome parents to walk with us to St. Mary’s Church leaving school at 9.15am. Please let the class teacher know if you are able to help.

Tuesday, 25th September from 1.45-3.30pm we will be opening our doors for the afternoon. All are welcome to visit our school to see what goes in our classrooms. This is especially relevant to any new parents wishing their children to start in September 2013.

Year 6 Trip To Hill Farm

Last Thursday Year 6 visited Hill Farm in Winscombe on a team building trip. There were several activities including a one mile walk. Mark in Year 6 said:

“You had to rely on the others in your team for help if you were to succeed at the activities”.

Year 4 Trip To Hill Farm

Connie and Sophie in Year 4 said they travelled on the coach to Winscombe Rugby Club and then walked along the Strawberry Line and through the dark tunnel. The activities they did included spiders web, grass skiing, orienteering and the water tube. They had fun and also met a dog called Humbug.
